Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Love Llama is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Love Llama processes the following data locally on your device:

  • Conversation text you paste or scan via screenshots
  • Photos you select for AI-generated image detection
  • Red flag checklist selections
  • Analysis history and results
All data is stored on-device using iOS Data Protection encryption (NSFileProtectionComplete). Your data is encrypted when your device is locked.

Third-Party Services

When you use certain features, data is sent to third-party AI services for analysis:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — Conversation text is sent for scam pattern analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.
  • Reality Defender — Photos are uploaded for AI-generated image detection. Reality Defender's privacy policy applies.

These services process data according to their own privacy policies. We only send the minimum data needed for analysis. No personal identifiers are included.

You must explicitly consent to this data processing before these features are enabled. You can revoke consent at any time from Settings.

API Keys

Your API keys for Anthropic and Reality Defender are stored securely in the iOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption (kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly). Keys are never sent to our servers or shared with any party other than their respective services.

Data Storage & Security

  • All analysis history is stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted at rest using NSFileProtectionComplete
  • No data is backed up to external servers
  • You can delete all data at any time from Settings

No Tracking or Analytics

Love Llama does not:

  • Track your activity
  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Use advertising identifiers
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use cookies or tracking pixels

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all stored data from Settings at any time
  • Remove API keys at any time
  • Revoke data processing consent at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Love Llama is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
